About Us

Driven by curiosity.
Grounded in reality.

Naruka AI Labs was born from a student's vision to bridge the gap between cutting-edge AI research and everyday utility. We are currently in our early development stages, dedicating our time to understanding complex AI architectures and translating them into usable tools.

Our philosophy is simple: AI shouldn't just be a novelty. It should be a practical companion that enhances human capability. We are actively building, learning, and iterating.

Our Vision

To build a suite of AI-powered products that act as an extension of the human mind—starting from accessible software tools today, and evolving into research for intelligent wearable technology tomorrow.
